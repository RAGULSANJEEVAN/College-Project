    SRI RAMACHANDRA

    INSTITUTE OF HIGHER EDUCATION AND RESEARCH

  • Sri Ramachandra Medical College and Research Institute was founded in 1985 by the Founder Chancellor, Shri Ramaswamy Udayar
  • Attained Deemed University status in 1994
  • Became Sri Ramachandra University in 2006
  • Sri Ramachandra Engineering and Technology was established in 2019 as the 14th Faculty of SRIHER
  • Dr. R. Vijarghavan is the Current Vice Chancellor of the University
  • Shri. V. R. Venkatachalam, Chancellor and Shri. R.V. Senguttivan, Pro Chancellor lead the university with more than 7000 student
