    International Center for Emerging Technologies

    Created in 2020, the International Center for Emerging Technologies (ICET) enables researchers within SRIHER and partner institutions in India and overseas to come together to study on wide range of technologies and their applications. Provisions exist for students to undertake collaborative Masters and Doctoral Programs with partner universities.

    Current areas of research interest are:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • AI in Business Applications
  • AI in Medicine, Dentistry and Pharmacy
  • AI in Ophthalmology
  • AI in Radiology
  • Automation in Healthcare
  • Bioinformatics
  • Biomedical Sciences
  • Block Chain
  • Automation in Manufacturing and Service Industries
  • Engineering and Medicine - Interdisciplinary Areas of Research
  • Engineering, Economics and Entrepreneurship
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning Applications
  • Cloud Computing
  • Cyber Security
  • Data Analytics
  • E-Commerce and ERP
  • Internet based studies
  • Technology Transfer and Policy Study
  • Robotics and Drones
  • Supply Chain Management
  • Social Issues and Technology
